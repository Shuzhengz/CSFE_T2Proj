About

This application is intended for UCSD undergraduates to showcase their academic and extracurricular progression. Users can additionally explore the profiles of their fellow undergraduates and interact with their posts and endeavors. Overall, our app is geared towards helping students discover new opportunities for involvement, strengthen their relationships with other UCSD undergrads, and build onto their existing skillset.
